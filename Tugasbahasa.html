Creamy Nutrijel Delight

Ingredients:

  1. 1 sachet Nutrijel (flavor of your choice)
  2. 500 ml Water
  3. Frisian flag white milk

Equipment:

  1. 1 Pot
  2. 1 Bowl
  3. Spoon for stirring

Preparation:
Gather all the ingredients and equipment needed.

Making the Nutrijel:
In a pot, combine the Nutrijel with 500 ml of water

Heat over medium heat, stirring continuously until the mixture dissolves and reaches a boil. Ensure there are no lumps.

Setting the Nutrijel:
Once boiling, remove the pot from heat and let the mixture cool slightly for about 5 minutes.

Pour the Nutrijel mixture into a pudding mold or bowl, filling it halfway.

Cooling:
Allow the Nutrijel to set at room temperature until it starts to firm up, then place it in the refrigerator for about 30 minutes.

Adding the Milk:
After the Nutrijel has set slightly, carefully pour 200 ml of Fisinflag White Milk over the top of the Nutrijel layer.
